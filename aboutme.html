About Me

I am a 3rd year student studying Philosophy, Politics and Economics at the University of Oxford. In my degree I am specialising in philosophy (Metaphysics, Epistemology, Ethics and Sartre) and economics (Microeconomics, Macroeconomics and Quantitative economics).

Outside of that through Code:First I've picked up some front-end development skills and am practising them by building this website from scratch using html, css, java, jquery and twitter bootstrap. Having worked at leading proptech startup WiredScore and helped launch app startup Compass I am committed to engaging further in coding and learn some back-end development so that I can become a fully fledged software developer.
